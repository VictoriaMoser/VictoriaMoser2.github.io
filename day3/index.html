Honduran Food

What is a country without their food and amazing flavours? To me nothing! Honduras typical dish is meat, refried beans, rice, plantains (green made into homemade chips or just sweet fried ones), avocado and a side of tortillas. We are also well known for our Baleadas. This exquisit food consists of a flour tortilla filled with refried beans, cheese (home made, not that processed shitt) and cream. This kind is called a Baleada Sencilla or Regular Baleada. You may also get it with eggs, meat (normally beef or chorizo), and avocado.
